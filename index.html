Choose Your Clean

Types of Upholstery Cleaning We Offer

We offer a range of flexible upholstery cleaning options to suit your furniture's condition, fabric type, and your specific requirements across Nairobi.

Hot Water Extraction (Steam) Cleaning

Our most popular upholstery cleaning method. Hot water extraction uses pressurised heated water injected deep into the fabric fibres, then immediately extracted along with dissolved dirt, bacteria, allergens, and staining agents. Ideal for heavily soiled sofas, fabric suite sets, and general deep cleaning. Results are thorough with a drying time of around 2–4 hours.

Deep Clean · Most Thorough
Low-Moisture Dry Cleaning

Ideal for delicate fabrics, antique upholstery, or any furniture that should not be saturated with water. We apply specialised dry-cleaning compounds that encapsulate dirt particles and are then extracted by powerful vacuuming. The fabric remains almost completely dry throughout, making this perfect for velvet, silk-blend, and vintage pieces. Furniture is typically ready to use immediately after treatment.

Delicate Fabrics · Quick Dry
Targeted Stain Removal

Have a stubborn stain on your sofa or armchair that you have not been able to shift? Our technicians apply professional-grade stain treatment agents selected specifically for the stain type — whether that is red wine, coffee, ink, grease, pet urine, or food. We treat, agitate, and extract to achieve the best possible result. Stain removal can be booked as a standalone service or as part of a full upholstery clean.

Stain-Specific · All Fabrics
Pet Odour & Allergen Treatment

Pet dander, saliva, and urine can embed deep into upholstery fibres, causing persistent odours and triggering allergies. Our specialist pet treatment combines enzymatic pre-treatment to break down organic matter with hot water extraction and a dedicated deodorising agent. The result is furniture that smells neutral and clean, with significantly reduced allergen levels — safe for both pets and people.

Pet-Friendly · Allergen Reduction

Common Questions About Our Upholstery Cleaning

How much does upholstery cleaning cost in Nairobi? ▾
Upholstery cleaning prices in Nairobi start from KES 1,500 per piece for armchairs and single seats, from KES 3,500 for a standard 3-seater sofa, and from KES 5,500 for a 3+2 suite. The final price depends on the size of the furniture, the fabric type, and the level of soiling. We provide free, written, itemised quotes with no obligation — call us on 0709 004 600 or WhatsApp for your personalised quote.
Is upholstery cleaning safe for all sofa fabrics? ▾
Yes, when done correctly. Our technicians assess each piece before selecting the appropriate cleaning method. We use eco-friendly, pH-balanced solutions suitable for cotton, linen, microfibre, velvet, chenille, and synthetic blends. For delicate or antique fabrics, we use a low-moisture dry-clean method to avoid any risk of damage, shrinkage, or colour bleeding.
How long does it take for upholstery to dry after cleaning? ▾
Most fabric sofas and chairs are dry within 2–4 hours after hot water extraction cleaning, depending on the fabric thickness and ventilation in the room. We recommend opening windows and using a fan to speed up the process. For our low-moisture dry-clean method, furniture is usually ready to use almost immediately after treatment.
Can you remove old or set-in stains from upholstery? ▾
We specialise in upholstery stain removal and can treat a wide range of stains including red wine, coffee, tea, grease, ink, pet urine, food, and general soiling. While the success of stain removal depends on the type of stain, how long it has been set, and the fabric involved, our professional treatments significantly reduce or eliminate most stains. We will always be honest with you about what is achievable before we start.

Do I need to move my furniture before you arrive? ▾
No — we clean all upholstery in situ. You do not need to move anything. Our technicians bring all the equipment needed and work around your furniture setup. We simply need clear access to the pieces being cleaned and a power socket nearby for our cleaning equipment.
