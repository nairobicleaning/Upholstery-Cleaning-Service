## Core Upholstery Cleaning Services

### 1. Vacuum Cleaning

High-powered vacuuming removes loose dirt, dust, and debris from fabric surfaces and hidden crevices.

### 2. Stain Removal Treatment

Specialized cleaning solutions are applied to break down tough stains without damaging the fabric.

### 3. Steam Cleaning

Steam cleaning uses high-temperature vapor to sanitize upholstery and remove deep-seated dirt.

### 4. Shampoo Cleaning

Foam-based cleaning agents are used to lift dirt and refresh upholstery materials.

### 5. Odor Removal and Sanitization

Deodorizing treatments eliminate unpleasant smells while sanitizing the fabric.

### 6. Fabric Protection Treatment

Protective coatings are applied to prevent future staining and prolong fabric life.
